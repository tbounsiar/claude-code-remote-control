Privacy Policy

Claude Code Remote Control — Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

Claude Code Remote Control ("the App") is a mobile application that lets you remotely interact with your Claude Code sessions. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

The App does not have a backend server. All data stays on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Session URLs — The Claude Code session URLs you add (via QR scan, paste, or auto-discovery).
  • Theme preference — Your chosen appearance setting (system, light, or dark).
  • Google Sign-In preference — Whether you have enabled the external browser mode.

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time from the Settings screen.

WebView & Authentication

The App loads claude.ai inside an embedded WebView (or your default browser if Google Sign-In mode is enabled). Your authentication with Claude is handled directly between your device and Anthropic's servers. The App does not intercept, read, or store your login credentials or authentication tokens.

Camera

The App requests camera permission solely to scan QR codes. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.

Push Notifications

Local push notifications may be used to alert you when a Claude Code session is waiting for input. These notifications are generated entirely on-device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App connects only to:

  • claude.ai and related Anthropic domains — to load your Claude Code sessions.
  • Google OAuth domains — only if you sign in with Google (handled by Anthropic's login flow, not by the App).

No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included in the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
